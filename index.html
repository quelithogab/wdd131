Haiti

Haiti, officially the Republic of Haiti, is a country located on the island of Hispaniola in the Caribbean Sea, east of Cuba and Jamaica, and south of the Bahamas. It occupies the western three-eighths of the island which it shares with the Dominican Republic. Haiti is the third largest country in the Caribbean with 27,750 km². The capital is Port-au-Prince.
